About me

My journey into programming began at the age of 10, inspired by my father and a Visual Basic programming book. This early exposure led me to explore Free Pascal and C in high school, and even create a library management system in Pascal. I pursued a bachelor's and master's in Computer Science, where I developed a deep love for programming. During my studies, I gained freelance experience as a full-stack developer. I later joined Tapsi, a leading taxi application in Iran, and then Balad, a major local map and navigation service with over 8 million users. At Balad, I progressed from software engineer to technical team lead, and eventually to senior software engineer, driven by a desire to help others and deepen my technical skills. My passion for teaching has also led me to instruct programming at school and start a YouTube channel.
